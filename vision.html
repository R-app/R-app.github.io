Welcome to
Relentless Offensive Attack
No matter who you are, someone out there is counting on you. At ROA we believe in developing physical skills and emotional resiliency that will aid us in a chaotic world. We believe in being prepared and want to share our journey with you. Perhaps you will find inspiration or strength in our story. Perhaps this will help you, and that person who is counting on you. You must be relentless to protect the things that matter most.
To be clear, we do not mean “it’s all about the journey, not the destination.” There is no destination, the journey it is never over. The path is one of self mastery and it requires effort every day. Large or small, whatever you can give represents the total contribution made towards your dream. It is time to commit and see what is possible.
